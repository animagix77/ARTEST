What's real vs. placeholder

Real: the cube system, the animation language, the four mechanics, the visual language. Each demo is a working interactive 3D scene that runs entirely in the browser.

Placeholder: patient identities, photography, voice talent, brand color tokens. Standing in until we have argenx brand guidelines, MLR-cleared content, and consented patient assets.

In production: swap placeholder portraits for documentary photography of real consented patients, replace synthesized voices with real recordings, brand-correct colors and typography to argenx spec, and wire the cubes to a CMS so each one can carry its own MLR-approved content payload.
