How to demo (90 seconds)

1
Open this page on a laptop and a phone. The laptop will display the AR marker; the phone will run the AR.
2
On the laptop, scroll down to the marker below and make it large on screen. (Or print it on plain paper — works either way.)
3
On the phone, open one of the four prototypes. Allow camera access when prompted. For Voice Cube, also tap the gold button to enable speech.
4
Point the phone camera at the marker. The AR experience plays on top of it. Hold steady — best results in good lighting, marker fully visible.
Important: WebAR requires HTTPS for camera access. Opening these files locally (file://) will not work — the prototypes must be served from an HTTPS URL. Ask Claude to deploy them to Vercel for a one-tap demo link, or run a local HTTPS server (e.g., npx serve -s . --ssl-cert cert.pem --ssl-key key.pem).

The marker

Scan this

This is the HIRO marker — the AR.js standard test pattern. The four prototypes are tracking it as a stand-in for the real campaign cube comp.

In production, this marker is replaced with the actual stacked-cube image from the campaign — meaning any printed cube (sales aid, journal ad, conference badge, leave-behind) becomes a scannable AR trigger. The HCP doesn't see a QR code; they see the campaign.

Tracking the brand image instead of HIRO requires a one-time NFT or MindAR descriptor compile — a sub-day production task once the cube comp is finalized.

What's real vs. placeholder

✓
Real: the AR mechanics. Image-target detection, 3D scene composition, animation orchestration, audio synthesis, the user flow on a phone — all working.
○
Placeholder: patient identities, photography, voice talent, brand color tokens, the cube target image. Standing in until we have argenx brand guidelines, MLR-cleared content, and consented patient assets.
→
What "production" looks like: swap HIRO for the real cube comp; replace the Unsplash placeholder portrait with documentary photography of a consented patient; commission real audio recordings; wire to a CMS so each printed cube can carry its own content payload.
