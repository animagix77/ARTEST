This demo runs on iPhone Safari.
AR Quick Look is iOS-native. To experience the portal, open this page on an iPhone in Safari, then tap the gold launch button. On Android or desktop, you can download the USDZ file to view in another tool, or watch a screen recording from the team.

How to demo

1
Tap the gold "Launch AR" button above. AR Quick Look opens fullscreen.
2
Move your phone slowly to scan the room. A reticle appears on the floor.
3
Tap the floor where you want the portal to land. The honey-gold cube places itself there.
4
Walk forward. The cube becomes a doorway. Step through. Look around inside the patient's morning.
5
Pinch to scale, drag to reposition, swipe up for the share/exit menu.
